3 Levels Of Search Engine Optimisation

Our approach to SEO is broken into three simple steps: Site architecture, content structure, and link building.

SITE ARCHITECTURE

A site’s architecture plays a critical role in how well search engines can crawl, read and index the information found on a given page. Ecommerce businesses need to understand how search engines rank information for specific search terms to best optimise their site.

LINK BUILDING

Search engines still rely heavily on authoritative and relevant external links pointing to websites as part of the search algorithm. External links pass link equity and positively impact organic growth.

CONTENT STRUCTURE

In an effective content SEO strategy, your keyword strategy and the way you structure your site work together. In a proper keyword strategy, you’ll have thought about common, competitive keywords as well as more long-tail niche search terms.
