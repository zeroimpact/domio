Why Paid?

Paid advertising drives legitimate business results by getting rid of ad waste. It aids in getting the most out of your time, money, and puts your efforts towards methods that actually work.

Paid search marketing is a critical part of your pay-per-click (PPC) campaign strategy. There is no better time to advertise than when someone already has determined they want your product or service and is actively looking to buy.

Our Work Process

A PPC process that’s equal parts transparent & effective.

  • 01. Research & Discovery

    Before we start optimising your paid search account, our consultants want to meet your team and learn everything about your company, target audience and business goals.

  • 02. DEVELOP STRATEGY

    There is a multitude of important factors in pay-per-click advertising in addition to the keywords you want to compete on. Where you want your budget allocated, geography you want to target.

  • 03. SETUP

    Once we decide on a strategy, our team will begin implementing it. The process starts with a structure of how your account followed by keyword research & creating the text ads potential visitors will see.

  • 04. Monitoring

    Identify low-performing areas of your ad campaigns fast. We will keep an eye on your campaign daily. If results begin to decline, we will detect it immediately and pause low-performing ads.

  • 05. Account Management

    Your company’s PPC account will have a manager, digital marketing consultant and digital marketing associate monitoring the results and optimising for better performance.

  • 06. Reporting

    Know how your paid search advertisements are performing. Your ad specialist will prepare monthly reporting for you to review. In addition, we will install conversion tracking on your advertisements.
