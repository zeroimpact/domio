Our Work Process

What's Included in Our Social Media Marketing Services.
Our process for social is similar to our approach to marketing in general:

"TRACK - TEST - TWEAK - REPEAT"

  • 01. Listen

    Your Company's vision is what shapes your future advertising campaigns. To get a clear understanding of what exactly you need, we'll run online interview sessions.

  • 02. Assign Manager

    A social media & ad manager will help you to run your social media advertisements, decrease costs and increase results. This person will learn your business, develop an advertising strategy and execute it.

  • 03. Develop Strategy

    We will develop a clear ad strategy for your business. It will include proven advertising funnels to show you how we plan on converting strangers into website traffic and conversions.

  • 04. Creating Ads

    Launch ads that people want to click on. Our ad specialists will write your copy, headlines, and develop images for your campaign. We will test your advertisements to identify which ads are driving the most results.

  • 05. Monitor & Optimize

    Identify low-performing areas of your ad campaigns fast. We will keep an eye on your campaign daily. If results begin to decline, we will detect it immediately and pause low-performing ads.

  • 06. Reporting

    Know how your paid search advertisements are performing. Your ad specialist will prepare monthly reporting for you to review. In addition, we will install conversion tracking on your advertisements.
